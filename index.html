About me

Hi, I'm Anu and I am driven by a fervent passion for storytelling in film and theatre.

My ambition is to bridge the gap between technology and storytelling. I am eager to partake in and pioneer innovative solutions at the nexus of technology and creative expression.

With a Bachelor of Computing (Computer Science) with Honours (Distinction) from the National University of Singapore, specializing in computer graphics, information retrieval, and artificial intelligence, I've developed a robust foundation in technology. I also completed the interdisciplinary University Scholars' Programme.

I started pursuing my ambition by working as a Software Engineer at Blackmagic Design, contributing to their flagship industry-standard post-production software DaVinci Resolve.

Starting this fall, I am set to undertake a Master of Science in Computer Animation and Visual Effects at Bournemouth University, and I'm thrilled to see where this leads.
